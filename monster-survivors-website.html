Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an exhilarating survival game where you'll face endless waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Your mission is simple: survive as long as possible while collecting power-ups, gaining experience, and upgrading your character's abilities.

With its addictive gameplay loop and progressive difficulty curve, Monster Survivors offers a perfect balance of challenge and reward. Each run is unique, with randomized enemy spawns and power-ups ensuring that no two games are ever the same.

Whether you have five minutes or an hour to spare, Monster Survivors provides an engaging experience that will keep you coming back for more. How long can you survive the monster onslaught?

Key Features

Endless Gameplay

Face wave after wave of monsters with increasing difficulty. Each game presents a unique challenge with no end in sight!

Character Progression

Level up your character, unlock new abilities, and customize your playstyle with various upgrades and power-ups.

Diverse Enemies

Battle against a wide variety of monsters, each with unique behaviors, attack patterns, and weaknesses to overcome.

Power-ups & Items

Collect power-ups and special items that enhance your abilities and give you the edge needed to survive longer.

Leaderboards

Compete with players worldwide to see who can survive the longest and achieve the highest score.

Accessible Controls

Easy to learn but hard to master controls that work perfectly on both desktop and mobile devices.

How to Play

Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Automatically attack nearby enemies. Collect experience orbs to level up and choose new abilities.

Objective

Survive as long as possible against endless waves of monsters. The longer you survive, the stronger you become – but so do your enemies!

Tips

Keep moving to avoid being surrounded. Prioritize collecting experience orbs to level up faster. Choose upgrades that complement your playstyle.
